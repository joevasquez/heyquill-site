Capabilities

What Quill actually does.

01

Dictate

MaciPhone
  • On-device transcription via Parakeet (Mac) or Whisper (Mac + iPhone), Core ML accelerated.
  • Hold a hotkey on Mac, hit record on iPhone — text pastes into whatever app you're in.
  • Optional Claude or ChatGPT polish: clean prose, email tone, structured notes, code comments.
  • Inline voice commands ("new paragraph", "period", "question mark").
02

Edit

Mac
  • Highlight any text in any app, trigger Quill, transform it in place.
  • Translate, shorten, expand, reformat as bullets, switch tone, fix grammar.
  • Use a preset or speak your own instruction.
  • Works in Messages, Mail, code editors, browsers, anywhere selectable text lives.
03

Act

MaciPhone
  • Quill detects your intent and proposes an action across your apps.
  • "Remind me to…" → Apple Reminders. "Add to my calendar…" → Calendar.
  • "Email…" → drafted in Mail. Action items → Todoist.
  • Always confirm before anything fires. No silent sends.
Privacy

Your audio never leaves your device.

Transcription runs locally on Mac and iPhone via Core ML. Edit and Action use your own Anthropic or OpenAI key — stored in Keychain — and only fire after you confirm. No telemetry, no analytics, no audio in the cloud.
